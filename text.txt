How many credits do you need to graduate from SoH? To graduate from the foundational years, you need a minimum of 20 credits and at least 3 credits during the advanced years. In the Foundational year Credits: Total Available: 39, Required to Graduate: 20 to pass, 25 for with merit, 30+ for with distinction. Advanced year Credits: Total Available: 30, Required to Graduate: 3 to pass, 4 for with merit, 5+ for with distinction.
